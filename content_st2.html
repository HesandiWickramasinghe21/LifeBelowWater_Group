Plastic Pollution in the Ocean

Millions of tons of plastic waste enter the oceans every year.It impacts marine species, ecosystems and even human health through the food chain

How it Affects Marine Life

Plastic entangles marine animals, is mistaken for food and breaks down into microplastics. Turtles, seabirds and whales are common victims.

What we can do

  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Participate in beach clean-ups
  • Support plastic bans and eco legislation
  • Educate others and spread awareness

The Future of Our Oceans

With global effort, innovation and education, we can reduce pollutions and protect marine ecosystem for future generations.
